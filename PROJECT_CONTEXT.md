# JITS Innovation Labs - Project Context

## 1. Overview
**JITS Innovation Labs** is a B2B technology partner specializing in enterprise-grade digital banking and fintech solutions. The company empowers financial institutions, microfinance organizations, retail, and enterprise businesses with scalable, secure, and cloud-native infrastructure to accelerate digital transformation.

## 2. Target Audience
- Retail Banking
- Microfinance Institutions
- E-Commerce Platforms
- Enterprise Businesses
- Healthcare & Education (Payment integrations)

## 3. Core Products & Solutions
The company offers a suite of modular, API-first fintech products:
- **Digital Banking:** Omnichannel banking experiences with modular architecture for retail and corporate banking.
- **Digital Wallet:** Secure, white-label wallet solution for seamless P2P, merchant payments, and loyalty programs.
- **Digital Lending:** Automated end-to-end digital lending with AI-driven risk assessment and fast approval workflows.
- **Cloud POS:** Cloud-based Point of Sale systems for seamless merchant transactions.
- **Customer Loyalty Wallet:** Specialized wallet solutions focused on customer retention, rewards, and loyalty points.
- **Fineract Core Banking:** Modernization of legacy systems with an API-first, highly scalable core banking engine.
- **Smart Vending Machine:** Integrated payment and management solutions for automated retail.

## 4. Value Proposition
- **Security & Compliance:** Bank-grade security protocols, end-to-end encryption, and strict regulatory compliance.
- **Unmatched Scalability:** Cloud-native, microservices architecture designed to handle millions of daily transactions.
- **Proven Delivery:** 100% success rate in enterprise-grade fintech implementations.
- **Deep Fintech Expertise:** Specialized domain knowledge in banking operations, payments, and financial regulations.

## 5. Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** React, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, Lucide React
- **Backend & Core:** Java, Node.js, NestJS, Go, Spring Boot
- **Infrastructure:** AWS, Kubernetes, Docker, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Oracle

## 6. Design Guidelines
- **Color Palette:** Deep Navy (slate-900), Cyan (cyan-500), Emerald (emerald-500), and Blue (blue-600).
- **Typography:** Inter (sans-serif) for clean, professional readability.
- **Vibe:** Trustworthy, enterprise-ready, modern, and secure.
- **Placeholders:** Images and specific content blocks are designed to be easily swappable by the content team.
